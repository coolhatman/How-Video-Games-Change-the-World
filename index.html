Game Changers: How Video Games Affect the World

What are Video Games?

Video games today are one of the most popular forms of entertainment. Today, there are approxomeitly 3.09 billion people who play video games. Video games are interactive electronic entertainment that involves using computers, gaming consoles, or mobile devices to play. They typically feature an audiovisual interface that immerses players in a virtual world, allowing them to control characters or elements within the game's environment. Video games come in different genres, such as action, adventure, role-playing, strategy, simulation, sports, and more. Whether players engage in challenges, puzzles, missions, or contests, their progress is often measured by reaching objectives, completing levels, or earning points.

Why are Video Games Played?

There are many reasons why video games are played. They include but are not limited to, entertainment, competition, stress relief, and education. Video games can also help improves things like dexterity and problem solving skills. It helps you engage with people more and can be an overall benifit to someone's life.

Nintendo in Hospitals

On August 12 2021, the Good News Network uploaded an article about how Nintendo is partnering with a non-profit to bring consoles to kids in hospitals. Starlight Nintendo Switch Gaming stations, have been making their way into hospitals.

The game station had games from Super Mario Party to The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. Since this was the time during the pandemic, the station was disinfected everytime it was used to prevent sickness. The children enjoyed playing their favortie video games, and not only by themsleves and they played with the other kids aswell. It is used to distract children when painful treatments, and it also helps them relaxe and help them pass time.

In 28 years, Starlight with Nintendo of America have provided millions of kids with help and comfort in hospitals.
